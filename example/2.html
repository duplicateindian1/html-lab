MS DHONI

MS Dhoni is a famous Indian cricketer and former captain. He is known for his cool attitude, good leadership and skills as a batsman and wicketkeeper. Under his captaincy, India won the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 Cricket World Cup and 2013 Champions Trophy. He is called "Captain Cool" and leads the Chennai Super Kings in IPL.

RECORDS OF MSD

  1. Only captain to win all three ICC trophies
  2. Most international matches as captain
  3. Most international stumpings
  4. Second most wins as captain
  5. Most runs at number 6 or lower
  6. Highest score by a wicketkeeper in ODIs
