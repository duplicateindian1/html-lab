MS DHONI

MS Dhoni is a legendary Indian cricketer and former captain known for his exceptional leadership, calm demeanor, and all-round skills as a right-handed batsman and wicketkeeper. Under his captaincy, India won the 2007 T20 World Cup, the 2011 Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy, making him the only captain to win all three major ICC tournaments. He is also known as "Captain Cool" and is the captain of the Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Records of MSD

  1. Only captain to win all three major ICC trophies
  2. Most international matches as captain
  3. Most international stumpings
  4. Second-most wins as captain
  5. Most runs at No. 6 or lower
  6. Highest individual score by a wicketkeeper in ODIs
